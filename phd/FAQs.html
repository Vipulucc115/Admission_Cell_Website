General Queries

  1. Where is LNMIIT located?

    LNMIIT is located in Jaipur at Rupa-ki-Nangal, Post: Sumel, Via: Jamdoli, Jaipur - 302031, Rajasthan, India. It is approximately 15 km from the heart of the city (Ajmeri Gate) and 11 km from Transport Nagar.

  2. How I shall reach to LNMIIT?

    It is located on the bypass that connects Jaipur-Agra Highway with Jaipur-Delhi Highway. This bypass can be reached via a link road connecting Jaipur-Agra Highway with Shishodia Rani-ka-Bagh, Keshav Vidypeeth, Jamdoli, and Sumel. For clarification, please click here:
    View Google Map

  3. How is the climate in Jaipur?

    The weather of Jaipur is semi-arid type. The summer season begins from April and continues till July. The temperature rises to an average of 300C. The city experiences monsoon showers in the months of August and September. With pleasant weather in the month of October, November to February are the months when city observe winters. The temperature ranges in between 5-150C during this season.

Admission-related Queries

    Details may be found here:

    https://admissions.lnmiit.ac.in/phd/Fee_Structure.html
  3. Will I receive any stipend/financial assistance during the programme?

    Yes, LNMIIT provides Teaching and Research Assistantship for candidates without any external fellowship, as well as partial financial support for attending conferences, medical benefits and insurance as per institute norms. More details here:

    https://admissions.lnmiit.ac.in/phd/Financial_Support.html
  4. I would like to apply for this programme. What is the first step?

    You can apply at this link with a nominal application fee and relevant documents:

    https://admissions.lnmiit.ac.in/phd/apply_now.html
  5. How can I ensure that I get admitted to a field of research of my choice?

    Please go through the research profiles of faculty members of your concerned department as outlined above. You can contact individual faculty members with overlapping interests and discuss with them in order to match with your preferences. Also please ensure that your application and Statement of Purpose clearly mention your preferred fields of research.

  6. What else should I keep in mind while applying?

    Please take care of the following:

    • You should state whether you are eligible for any external fellowship. If so, please attach the certificate.
    • If your post-graduate result is in CGPA, please provide the conversion certificate (CGPA to percentage).
    • Please mention whether you are pursuing or have finished your post-graduate degree.
