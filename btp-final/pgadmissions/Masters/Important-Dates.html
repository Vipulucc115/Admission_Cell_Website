Important Dates

LNMIIT offers M.Sc. in Physics and Mathematics. Admissions to M.Sc. will be in the following modes:

1.JAM Mode: Joint Admission test for Masters (JAM)- 2024 qualified students will be admitted directly to the Institute subject to the merit list and availability of seats. (Note that the percentile should be above the cut-off score for the general category.)

Important Dates*

Event Dates
Online application form opens 20 March 2024
Last date for filling online application form 15 May 2024
Result Declaration by 24 May 2024
Last date of payment of fee via JAM Mode 10 June 2024

2. CUET-PG Mode: Central University Entrance Test (CUET-PG)-2024 exam qualified students will be admitted directly to the Institute subject to the merit list and availability of seats. (Note that the cut-off for the same will be decided post the last date of application via this mode.)

Important Dates*

Event Dates
Online application form opens 20 March 2024
Last date for filling online application form 15 June 2024
Result Declaration by 20 June 2024
Last date of payment of fee via CUET PG Score Mode 01 July 2024

3. Institute Online Admission Mode:. Candidates can apply via the institute mode if

The candidate does not have a JAM-2024/ CUET-PG-2024 score card.

JAM-2024/ CUET-PG-2024 qualified candidates have missed the last date to fill the form in their respective mode. (Note: JAM-2024/ CUET-PG-2024 score will be null and void in this mode)

Institute admission procedure will be via online interview. The merit list will be prepared on the performance of the candidate in the interview only. Candidates will be admitted based on this merit list subject to availability of seats.

Important Date*

Event Dates
Online application form opens 20 March 2024
Last date for filling online application form 03 July 2024
Date of Online Interviews: 09/10 July 2024
Result Declaration by 15 July 2024
Last date of payment of fee Institute mode 20 July 2024

Note: The date of joining for all the modes will be common for the first round of admission and it will be decided later.

*LNMIIT reserves the right to change the dates depending on unforeseen reasons and the same will be updated on the website.
