General Queries

  1. Where is LNMIIT located?

    LNMIIT is located in Jaipur at Rupa-ki-Nangal, Post: Sumel, Via: Jamdoli, Jaipur - 302031, Rajasthan, India. It is approximately 15 km from the heart of the city (Ajmeri Gate) and 11 km from Transport Nagar.

  2. How shall I reach LNMIIT?

    It is located on the bypass that connects Jaipur-Agra Highway with Jaipur-Delhi Highway. This bypass can be reached via a link road connecting Jaipur-Agra Highway with Shishodia Rani-ka-Bagh, Keshav Vidypeeth, Jamdoli, and Sumel. For clarification, please click here: https://admissions.lnmiit.ac.in/ugadmissions/howtoreach.html
    View Google Map

  3. How is the climate in Jaipur?

    The weather in Jaipur is semi-arid type. The summer season begins in April and continues till July. The temperature rises to an average of 30 ° C with the maximum temperature of 44° C. The city experiences monsoon showers in the months of August and September. With pleasant weather in the month of October, November to February are the months when the city observes winters. The temperature ranges between 5-15°C during this season.

  3. How is the laboratories infrastructure?
    • State-of-the-art laboratories have been set up for both undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) level courses.
    • Some of the important UG laboratories are: Basic Electronics Lab, Basic Physics Lab, E-CAD Lab, Analog Communication Lab, Digital Communication Lab, Signal Processing Lab, Microwave Lab, Micro-controller and Micro-processor Lab, Data Base Management Systems (DBMS) Lab, Operating Systems Lab, Programming Lab, Data Structures Lab, Networking Lab, CAD Lab.
    • In addition to this, we have the following laboratory for the PG level courses: Advanced Instrumentation Lab, Synthesis Lab, Wireless Lab, Simulation Lab, to expose the students to the latest research in different areas of science and engineering.
    • IBM Laboratory with tools for Big Data & Analytics and Mobile Application Development tools like Cognos Insight, InfoSphere BigInsight, Worklight.
    • National Instruments (NI) laboratory with myRIO, cRIO and sbRIO hardwares along with a wide range of sensors and actuators for research and robotic applications.
    • Texas Instruments (TI) laboratory with latest kits including Beaglebone Black, D8500, TIVA and Atmel Dragon Board Xmega and Rapsberry Pi Boards.
    • High Performance Computing facility by HP for research purposes. Mechanical workshop with conventional workshop and laboratories and NC-CNC machines.

Hostel and Other infrastructure

  1. What type of hostel accommodation do you provide to students?

    Although LNMIIT is a residential institute, in view of COVID-19 specific physical distancing norms, hostels would not be mandatory and accommodation in the hostel would be subject to availability.

  2. What medical facilities are available to students admitted at LNMIIT?

    Institute offers medical facilities for its students, teaching, and non-teaching staff living in the campus. This includes a Resident Doctor, a Resident Nurse, Emergency care, Physio-therapy unit, Ambulance Service, tie-up to a nearby advanced hospital for immediate referral/treatment. The institute facilitates a group medical insurance policy for all the students.

  3. What is the arrangement for food?

    The institute has two centralized mess facilities for providing breakfast, lunch, evening snacks, and dinner.

  4. Is internet facility available in the campus?

    Internet facility is available in all the hostels and the academic area through wired and wi-fi connection.
