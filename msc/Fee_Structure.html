Fee Structure

Note: The fee structure for the academic year 2024-25 will be updated soon.

The fee structure for the academic year 2023-24 is given below

The application fee (non-refundable) for the M. Sc. courses is INR 500. For women applicants, the application fee is INR 250.

A Caution Money (One time deposit; Refundable at the end of the Programme; No interest is payable): INR 10,000
B Semester Fee
Registration Fee INR 3,000
Tuition Fee INR 23,600
Sub Total (B) (Without the caution Money) INR 26,600

Hostel and Mess charges per semester (in INR)

(Staying in the Hostel is optional. Hostel facility is subjected to availability of the rooms.)

1 Hostel charges 25,000
2 Electricity Charges 6,600
3 Internet Charges 500
4 Student’s activity charges 1,500
5 Mess Food Charges (Adjustable as per actual basis at the end of the Semester/ Academic year) 18,000
Sub Total 51,600

Note:

  1. The institute reviews the fee structure & charges periodically. There will be an annual increase of approximately 10% in Fee/charges.
  2. All charges are mandatory nothing can be waived off.
  3. Medical cum accidental insurance yearly charges shall be charged on actual basis with 2nd- semester fee.
  4. Alumni fee: Lifetime membership for LNMIIT alumni association of INR 2,000/- (one time) will be charged from all graduating students.
  5. Same fee shall be charged in 2nd semester.
  6. Summer/Winter course fees shall be paid separately as decided (if opted).
  7. Electricity charges may also change if any change in the Govt. tariff/JVVNL tariff charges proposed anytime in future.
  8. Bus charges shall be payable as per use as decided by the Institute.
  9. If hostel opted mess is compulsory.
